§ 02 — Where the words happen

Caucus vs. Regular vs. everything else.

Caucus meetings — the pre-meeting deliberative sessions — host slightly more total commissioner speech than the official Regular meetings where votes are cast. The mix varies sharply by commissioner.

How each commissioner divided their time
